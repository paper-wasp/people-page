Red Hat Central Region Lunch and Learns are Back!

By now the Red Hat Solution Architect Teams were hoping that we could be meeting face-to-face and be back on a regular Red Hat User Group cadence. Unfortunately, that has not been the case in many of our locales.

For this run we are going to dedicate 5 weeks to a single topic and that is Ansible Automation Platform, specifically AAP 2. In this series we are going to take you through the platform showing you the new features, capabilities and benefits of the platform.
